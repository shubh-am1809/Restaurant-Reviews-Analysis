# Task: Restaurant Reviews Analysis

# Objective:
Analyze customer review text to identify common positive and negative
keywords, calculate average review length, and explore the relationship
between review length and restaurant rating.

# Approach:
- Cleaned and tokenized review text
- Separated positive and negative reviews based on rating
- Extracted most frequent keywords
- Calculated review length and compared it with ratings

# Conclusion:
Positive reviews commonly contain words related to quality and service,
while negative reviews emphasize issues such as delay or poor taste.
Higher-rated restaurants tend to have longer, more detailed reviews.
